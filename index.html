About Us

VETI started as a small company way back in 1994 as a small scale Computer Repair shop, because of the demand of the retail computer parts, the company thought of engaging in Computer Sales, where the company enjoyed almost the largest share in the computer industry in Cabanatuan City. In the year 1997, the company started servicing different companies both private and government companies, educational institutions all over Nueva Ecija.
